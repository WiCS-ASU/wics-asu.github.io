WiCS 14th Annual Programming Competition

March 17th, 2018

Come and be a part of this year’s annual programming competition! Put your programming skills to the test and compete against your fellow students for a chance to win some awesome prizes and get an opportunity to network with representatives from our sponsors!

How to Sign Up

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Registration will take place during the weeks of February 26th to March 2nd in the BYAC lobby.
A $15 deposit is required at registration, and will be returned back to you after the competition. Please pay with cash, check, or Venmo.
