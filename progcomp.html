WiCS 15th Annual Programming Competition

March 16th, 2019


Come and be a part of this year’s annual programming competition! Put your programming skills to the test and compete against your fellow students for a chance to win some awesome prizes and get an opportunity to network with representatives from our sponsors!

Every current ASU student, regardless of gender and experience level, is invited to participate. There will be prizes for top Overall, top Freshman, top Sophomore, top Female/Non-Binary, as well as a raffle! Prizes include Nintendo Switches, iPads, Amazon Echo Dots, Gift Cards and more! As always, there will be free lunch, t-shirts, and swag!

SAMPLE PROBLEMS

Your team's answer will only be accepted in either Java, C, or C++

How to Sign Up

- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

  • Registration will take place at the registration table during the weeks of February 26th to March 15th in the BYAC lobby.

  • Not everyone from your team has to be present to register. Teams can consist of up to 3 members (meaning it is possible to sign up individually or as a couple as well), with a maximum of 1 graduate student per team. If you want help finding a team please email us.

  • Deposits can be made at the ProgComp KickOff on Wednesday, February 28th, 2018 from 6-7:30 pm, or at the registration table in BYAC the week after spring break until all slots are filled.
    YOUR TEAM IS NOT FULLY REGISTERED UNTIL WE HAVE YOUR DEPOSIT. We must have your deposit ($15) by 5:00 PM on Thursday, March 15th.

  • A $15 deposit is required at registration, and will be returned to you at the competition. Please pay with cash, check, or venmo at our registration table.

  • Checks should be made payable to “Women In Computer Science”. Alternatively, we are accepting payments on Venmo to @wics-asu (in the comments leave your team name).

  • It is your responsibility to make sure you get a receipt when dropping off the deposit. On the day of the competition, you will need to bring your receipt and student IDs.

The number of participants is limited to a first come, first serve basis.
